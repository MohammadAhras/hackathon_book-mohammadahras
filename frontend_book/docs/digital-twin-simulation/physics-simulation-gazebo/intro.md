# Physics Simulation with Gazebo

Welcome to the Physics Simulation with Gazebo chapter! This section will teach you about gravity, collisions, and dynamics in Gazebo physics simulation, as well as world and robot simulation basics and the role of Gazebo in robotics testing.

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this chapter, you will be able to:
- Understand the core concepts of physics simulation in Gazebo
- Configure gravity, collision detection, and dynamic simulation
- Create basic simulation environments with proper physics properties
- Explain the role of Gazebo in robotics testing and validation

## Chapter Overview

Gazebo is a physics-based simulation environment that provides realistic simulation of robots in complex indoor and outdoor environments. It provides:

- Accurate physics simulation with gravity, collisions, and dynamics
- Realistic world modeling with lighting and environmental effects
- Robot simulation with URDF integration
- Sensor simulation capabilities

This chapter covers:

1. **Gravity, Collisions, and Dynamics** - The fundamental physics concepts
2. **World and Robot Simulation Basics** - Creating and configuring simulation environments
3. **Role of Gazebo in Robotics Testing** - How Gazebo enables safe testing of robot algorithms
